INDIAN ARMY
The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces.
The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Army, and its professional head is the Chief of Army Staff, who is a four-star general. Wikipedia Headquarters: New Delhi
Motto(s): सेवा परमो धर्म: (Sanskrit); Sevā Paramo Dharmaḥ (ISO); transl. "Service Before Self"
Size: : 1,237,117 active personnel; 960,000 reserve personnel; ~296 manned aircraft
Founded: 26 January 1950; 73 years ago (as current service) 1 April 1895; 128 years ago (first established)
Colours: Gold, red and black
Part of: Indian Armed Forces
Subsidiary: Army Welfare Housing Organisation
